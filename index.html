Hao Wei

I am a PhD student at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), advised by Prof. Nils Thuerey in the Physics-based Simulation group. I am passionate about building AI systems that respect and exploit physical laws — particularly for simulating complex fluid dynamics efficiently and accurately.

My research focuses on neural PDE solvers, with an emphasis on designing architectures with strong physical inductive biases (e.g., rotational equivariance) and learning-based correctors for hybrid numerical solvers.

Previously, I received my master's degree from Zhejiang University, and interned at Microsoft Research Asia and the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.
